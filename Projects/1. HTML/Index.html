When and where were burgers invented?

The origin of the hamburger is not exactly known. However, one theory suggests that the name "hamburger" comes from the seaport town of Hamburg, Germany, where it is thought that 19th-century sailors brought back the idea of raw shredded beef (known today as beef tartare) after trading with the Baltic provinces of Russia.
